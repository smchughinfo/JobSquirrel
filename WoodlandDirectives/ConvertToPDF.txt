Convert CURRENT_RESUME_DIRECTORY/resume.html to a pdf using /HelperScripts/GenerateResume.bat. You will have to run it from windows, not wsl, which is why the batch script is provided (to bridge the gap).

For Example: 

cmd.exe /c "C:\Users\seanm\Desktop\JobSquirrel\HelperScripts\GenerateResume.bat ResumeData\resume1.html ResumeData\resume1.pdf"
